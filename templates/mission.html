Our Mission

Preserving Hindi heritage through conversation

"To bridge the language gap for children growing up in non-Hindi speaking environments, creating a safe and engaging space for them to practice their heritage language through natural conversation."

Why We Exist

This project was born from a heartfelt family dinner conversation about maintaining heritage language skills while growing up abroad. We witnessed firsthand how children in the Indian diaspora struggle to maintain their connection with Hindi in predominantly non-Hindi speaking environments.

Created specifically for young learners like our nephew in Singapore, who was struggling to maintain his Hindi skills in a predominantly Chinese-speaking environment, we realized that traditional language learning apps weren't enough. Children needed something more natural, more conversational, more like chatting with a caring friend or family member.

Our Approach

Unlike traditional language learning applications that focus on structured lessons and formal grammar exercises, we believe in the power of natural conversation. Our AI tutor acts like a motherly figure who's genuinely curious about your child's day, creating an environment where Hindi flows naturally.

We understand that children learn best when they're engaged and having fun. That's why our conversations are child-led, flowing naturally based on their interests – whether that's talking about school, dinosaurs, cartoons, or their favorite activities.

Our Vision

We envision a world where heritage languages thrive across generations. Where children growing up in multicultural environments can maintain strong connections to their linguistic roots while embracing their global communities.

Our goal is to make Hindi speaking practice as natural and enjoyable as talking to a beloved family member, helping preserve the rich linguistic heritage of Hindi while building confidence in young speakers.

The Impact We're Making

Every conversation our AI tutor has with a child is a step towards preserving Hindi for future generations. We're not just teaching a language; we're maintaining cultural connections, building confidence, and ensuring that the beauty of Hindi continues to flourish in diaspora communities worldwide.

Through gentle correction, encouragement, and engaging dialogue, we're helping children develop not just language skills, but a love for their heritage language that will last a lifetime.
